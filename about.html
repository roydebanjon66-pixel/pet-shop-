Our Beginning

We Started Because We Love Pets

PawsHome was founded in 2019 by two animal lovers who were frustrated by the lack of quality, safe, and affordable pet products in the market. We believe every pet — whether a playful puppy, a sleepy cat, a chirpy bird, or a dazzling fish — deserves the very best care.

What started as a small online store has grown into a community trusted by over 10,000 pet families worldwide. We carefully curate every product we sell, working only with brands that share our commitment to animal health and safety.

💚
